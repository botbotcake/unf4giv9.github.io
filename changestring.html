How To: Change String

Click Here For Another Guide

Step 1:


Open the apkeditor application.


Step 2:


Click on Select Apk From App, and select the app you would like to edit.


Step 3:


Select the option that says "full edit" and wait for everything to load up.


Step 4:


Click on the search bar near the bottom and search the string(text) you would like to change and click find.

Once it appears, click on it and change it to what you want then click Ok.



Step 5:


You then need to select build in the top right and wait for it to complete. Once it completes, click remove -> Uninstall then Install it, and it should be good.
